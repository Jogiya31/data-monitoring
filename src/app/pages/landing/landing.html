What is PRAYAS?

PRAYAS gives programme-monitoring teams a single, real-time window into the health of scheme data submissions. Instead of chasing spreadsheets, teams see exactly which schemes are late, which values look abnormal, and where intervention is needed — across every level of government.

Everything a monitoring user needs

Key features

Delay & defaulter dashboard

KPIs, frequency analysis, grace tracking and a full detail table for every scheme.

Department-wise reporting

Delay rates and average delay rolled up by department, ministry, state and district.

Anomaly analysis

Zero, consecutive-zero, negative, cumulative-dip and abnormal-spike detection.

Reports & analytics center

17 standard reports with multi-select bulk export to Excel and CSV.

Alerts & notifications

Severity-ranked, searchable alert stream with acknowledge, snooze and export.

Powerful filtering

Multi-select Sector, Department, State, District, Scheme, KPI and free-text search.

Bilingual & responsive

English & Hindi throughout, on desktop, tablet and mobile.

Live or sample data

Connects to the monitoring API, with a built-in sample dataset for demos.
